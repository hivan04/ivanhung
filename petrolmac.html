2021 Petrol Crisis

Objective - Produce an advertisement-like video on why someone should study the subject Economics.


This was my second group project at University and the objective was fairly broad, making it quite a challenge to narrow down on what we should talk about in the 8 minutes. We decided to talk about the issues which arised from this event, talk about the short and long run consequences from it and potential solutions to resolving the issue (to prevent hysterisis).

Personally I enjoyed doing the research of the project and helped me to enhance both my leadership and researching skills - I enjoyed completing this project; despite the usage of iMovie once again! Hope you enjoy my presentation (which can be found below)!
